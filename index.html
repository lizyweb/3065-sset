Vision and Mission

Vision

Zero Poverty is a concept and initiative aimed at eradicating poverty entirely. It's a bold vision that acknowledges the fundamental human right to a decent standard of living, which includes access to adequate food, clean water, shelter, education, and healthcare. This initiative recognizes that poverty is not just about lack of income but also encompasses various factors such as social exclusion, lack of access to resources, and discrimination.

Mission

In India, ensuring education for all is a significant challenge due to the country's vast population, diverse demographics, and complex socio-economic dynamics. While India has made strides in expanding access to education over the years, significant disparities still exist, particularly in terms of quality and equity. Here are some key aspects related to education for all in India:

Universal Access
Equity
Skill Development
Digital Divide
Higher Education
Quality Improvement
Public-Private Partnerships
