SUSILASUBRAMANIAN EDUCATIONAL TRUST (SSET)

SusilaSubramanian Educational Trust (SSET) is a dedicated voluntary and non-governmental organization established in 2020. Committed to addressing ignorance, illiteracy, and social deprivation, SSET strives to uplift disadvantaged communities by meeting their physical, mental, educational, and social needs. Together, we work towards a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
